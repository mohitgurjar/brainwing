BOT Series

The Aeronavics BOT Ti-QR is a cross hybrid CX and VM with the latest Titanium QR components. It is compact, it will pack down into a small day pack and swing up to 14" carbon props. A serious little kit powerful enough to carry a Point and Shoot camera for 20-25 minutes and comes in under 4kg maximum take-off weight. A retro pocket rocket and a perfect solution for aerials-on-the-go. 670mm diameter suited to fitting up to 14" propellers.

Core BOT Ti-QR

The core of your BOT craft consists of high quality carbon fiber center plates and comes complete with flight control adaptor plate, rear stacking plates, front body bracket and other extras. Industrial grade graphite fiber filled composite internal boom mounting create a rock solid construction for your BOT.

Booms and Engine Mounting BOT

The booms for the BOT are made of industrial strength carbon fiber 3k twill tube with high-end matt finish and allow for up to 14" props with their 280mm length. These tubes offer durability and natural vibration absorption properties. This kit now includes the new aluminium engine mount disks that support Tiger Motors. Engine mounts boots made from the same material are also included for cosmetic appeal and to protect your engine wires in case of a mishap. The complete content of this kit can be found in the build manuals.

CX-600 and BOT Landing Gear Extended Ti

Quick release landing gear with snap on skids for easy pack down. Ground clearance is 160mm. Now with Battery Mounting Bracket and Camera mounting bracket.

Dome 185mm Carbon Fiber Finish + Fittings

Dome 185mm diameter with Carbon Fiber patterned finish for the BOT. Made from high impact UV resistant polycarbonate. Also fits AD and VM series craft.

Welcome to 'BrainWing'

We are a bunch of young people deeply passionate about RC machines and Films. Hence, we combined our two passions into an interesting profession that we love doing everyday. We like flying and have been doing this since over 6 years. Over the period, we have gained extensive knowledge about different products and have always updated ourselves with the latest and the best machines out in the market. As we grow, we try to stock more products gradually.

At BrainWing, we specialize in providing Aerial Services to Films and Entertainment Industry. We proudly express our expertise in Aerial Cinematography with our range of work over the years. We work for Feature Films, corporate movies, documentary, TV's etc. To know more about out work, please visit the Gallery page.

BrainWing is our child and we opt to deliver the best machines to all the flying enthusiats through this. We hope to live up to our customer's expectations ans provide them with the best customer experience ever.

HAPPY FLYING!

SkyJib Series

SkyJib is the Aeronavics Signature Pro Camera Ship purposefully and carefully designed to deliver an ultra-strong airframe package with maximum performance and inflight characteristics. A feature packed craft with state-of-the-art components - preferred choice of RED as featured on their stand at the NAB. Depending on your payload requirements choose your core configuration and further customize your craft on the next page. All ins & outs of the SkyJib Series features you can find in our showroom. Full setup suggestions are offered in our products guide or in summarized form in our download section and a full specification of parts can be found in the build manuals.

SkyJib 8 Ti-QR

The SkyJib-8 is THE Hollywood Heavy Lifter and by far the most popular Pro camera ship worldwide. It has assisted in the making of many blockbusters with confidence and flair. Its charismatic presence and impressive exterior match its outstanding capabilities of producing smooth sweeping shots with cameras such as the RED Epic. Plenty of time to frame your shot as it will carry your Pro camera for up to 15 minutes. 1160mm diameter - suited for fitting up to 15" propellers, also available in 'Retractable Landing Gear' version - SJR.

Core SkyJib-8 Ti-QR

The core of your SkyJib-8 Ti-QR craft consists of high quality carbon fiber center plates and comes complete with flight control adaptor plate. On strategic places rivnuts are fitted for assisting easy pack down once you have build your full craft for transport and travel. Industrial grade graphite fiber filled composite internal boom mounting create a rock solid construction for your SkyJib-8 Ti-QR.

Booms & Engine Mounting SkyJib-8 Titanium 540

Complete booms and engine mount kit for the SkyJib 8 Titanium 540mm.

SkyJib Landing Gear and Gear Rail Assembly Retractable Ti

Quick release landing gear/gear rail assembly with increased ground clearance for fitting large camera mounts and Cinema camera like the RED Epic.

SkyJib-8 Crash and Battery Cage + Dome 210mm Tint

The SkyJib-8 Crash Cage offers optimum protection for your flight electronics and extra bracing to the center section of the craft adding overall strength to the construction of this heavy lifter. With the large Tinted patterned high impact UV resistant polycarbonate dome fitted underneath the crash cage, your electronics will be well protected. Now with two Crash Cage Battery Mounting Trays, allowing you to strap your batteries directly to the crash cage. Offering a safer and stabler configuration.

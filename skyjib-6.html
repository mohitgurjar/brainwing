SkyJib Super-6 Ti-QR

The SkyJib Super-6 can fit up to 17" propellers within an overall diameter of 1080mm and is a true DSLR Dream Ship. Very smooth, very stable and VERY good-looking. The retractable landing gear offers full 360 clear views.

Core SkyJib-6 Ti-QR

The core of your SkyJib-6 Ti-QR craft consists of high quality carbon fiber center plates and comes complete with flight control adaptor plate. On strategic places rivnuts are fitted for assisting easy pack down once you have build your full craft for transport and travel. Industrial grade graphite fiber filled composite internal boom mounting create a rock solid construction for your SkyJib-6 Ti-QR.

SkyJib Landing Gear and Gear Rail Assembly Retractable Ti

Quick release landing gear/gear rail assembly with increased ground clearance for fitting large camera mounts and Cinema camera like the RED Epic.

SkyJib-X4 Crash and Battery Cage + Dome 210mm Tint

The SkyJib-X4 Crash Cage offers optimum protection for your flight electronics and extra bracing to the center section of the craft adding overall strength to the construction of this heavy lifter. With the large Tinted patterned high impact UV resistant polycarbonate dome fitted underneath the crash cage, your electronics will be well protected. Now with two Crash Cage Battery Mounting Trays, allowing you to strap your batteries directly to the crash cage. Offering a safer and stabler configuration.

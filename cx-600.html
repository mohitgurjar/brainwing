Aeronavics CX 600 The Compact Workhorse

  1. Home
  2. Aeronavics CX 600

CX Series

The Aeronavics CX-600 Ti is compact and stylish and suitable for Point and Shoot, GoPro and thermal imaging cameras such as FLIR. Its enclosed and customizable body has character and keeps the flight electronics protected from light rain and dust. Its compact size with quick release snap-off landing gear makes it an easy transportable unit and a very appealing yet versatile field machine. 670mm diameter suited to fitting up to 14" propellers.

Core CX

The core of your CX craft consists of high quality carbon fiber center plates and comes complete with flight control adaptor plate, rear stacking plates, front body bracket and other extras. Industrial grade graphite fiber filled composite internal boom mounting create a rock solid construction for your CX.

Booms 310mm & Engine Mounting CX-600 Titanium

The booms for the CX-600 Titanium are made of industrial strength carbon fiber 3k twill tube with high-end matt finish and allow for up to 14? props with their 310mm length. These tubes offer durability and natural vibration absorption properties.

CX-600 and BOT Landing Gear Extended Ti

Quick release landing gear with snap on skids for easy pack down. Ground clearance is 160mm. Now with Battery Mounting Bracket and Camera mounting bracket.

CX-600 Ti Body

This fully enclosed canopy is made of UV stable high impact resistant polycarbonate, protecting your flight electronics against light rain or snow and potential mishaps. The underbelly has slotted areas for airflow and cooling and mounting electronics components or extra battery power. Its aerodynamic shape gives the craft caracter and this default clear body allows for custom branding and a personal touch.
